Types of Computers

The key learning objectives for this simulation are:

  • List the Various Types and Characteristics of Personal Computers
  • Give Examples of Other Personal Computing Devices

In the business world, different employees working for the same company often use different types of computer systems because of the different responsibilities and needs of his or her job.

In this simulation, you will decide which computer system BEST serves the needs of a specific employee, based on their job description and what you’ve learned about PCs, Macs, desktops, notebooks, and tablets.
